St. Paul's Church
Hamburg, Minnesota

Hello! We're glad you stopped by!

We're St. Paul's Evangelical Reformed Church of Hamburg, Minnesota. We're not a big church, but we're a strong church, and over the past century and a half of our existence we've learned a lot about how to love people the way the Lord Jesus Christ called us to do. And we've also learned that being trendy isn't all it's cracked up to be. Instead, we have embraced the deep traditions of our Christian history and committed ourselves to speaking the eternal Truth of the Bible.


If you have a hunger in your heart to know God in a deep way, we hope you will join us. We believe that we can show you the way to let the Lord Jesus Christ fill that God-shaped hole in your heart. We believe that we can help you grow stronger and deeper in your faith. We believe that we can show you how to have confidence that God is real, and that you truly belong to Him. We have experienced God's love in our lives, and we want to bring you alongside us as you experience it for yourself.
